Tapenade (Olive Spread)

This is a really simple dish to prepare and it's always a big hit at parties. My father recommends:

"Make this the night before so that the flavors have time to blend. Jsut bring it up to room temperature before you serve it. In the winter, try serving it warm"

Ingredients

  • 1 8oz. jar sundried tomaltoes
  • 2 large garlic cloves
  • 2/3c. kalamata olives
  • 1 t. capers

Instructions

Compine tomatoes an garlicin the food. Blend until as smooth as possible.
Add caper and olives. Pulse the motor a few times until they are incorporated, but still retain some texture.
Servr on thin toast rounds with goat cheese and fresh basil garnish (optional)
